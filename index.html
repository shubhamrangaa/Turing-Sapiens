Who we are

We are a student community set to develop the tech culture.

We the students of Manipal University Jaipur have come together to put forward our proposal to start a new Technical Club for the college. Our vision is to collaborate with the best Information Technology MNCs and start-ups in India and offer our students the best platforms to develop their skills. We also aim to provide a mentorship program to acquaint and familiarize our students with the platform, frameworks and technologies at the grassroots level, and prepare them for the road ahead.

This tech-based club will provide a place for the introduction of cohort study regarding principles and practices that are the foundation of understanding how technology and engineering support each other as well as how they work.
